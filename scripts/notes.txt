The researcher is treating network conditions as the independent experimental variable and measuring 
how different approximate nearest neighbor (ANN) architectures respond in terms of retrieval performance 
and energy consumption.

Key ANN algorithmic architectures include:
Graph-Based Methods i.e. HNSW, NSW 
Quantization-Based Methods i.e. PQ and ScaNN
Tree-Based Methods i.e. KD tree, & Annoy (Approximate Nearest Neighbors Oh Yeah) 
Clustering and Hashing Methods i.e. Inverted File Index (IVF), and Locality-Sensitive Hashing (LSH). 

Libraries include: 
FAISS, Annoy, ScaNN, ChromeDB
